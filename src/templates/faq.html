Frequently Asked Questions

How is my personal data handled?

We firmly believe that you own your data, not us. We never sell your personal data to third parties and we use industry-standard best practices, making sure your private information is always encrypted and secure. We are also happy to delete your data upon request.

How can I change my password?

You can change your password at any time by following these steps:
1. login using your current email address
2. click “My Account” in the top right
3. select Settings from the dropdown
4. select “Change your account login”
5. enter your current password
6. enter your new password and then reenter your new password to confirm
7. select “save”
Just remember: the next time you log into the website, you'll want to use your new password to log in.
